COVID19 Update

The SARS-CoV-2 virus causes coronary artery disease (COVID-19). The majority of persons who are infected with the virus will have mild to severe respiratory illness and recover without the need for therapy. Some, on the other hand, will become critically unwell and require medical help. People over the age of 65, as well as those with underlying medical diseases such as cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic respiratory disease, or cancer, are at a higher risk of developing serious illness. COVID-19 can make anyone sick, causing serious illness or death, at any age.
